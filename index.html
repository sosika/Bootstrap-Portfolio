About Me

Skilled in Computer Network Operations, Internet Protocol Suite (TCP/IP), Analytical Skills, Technical Support, and Voice over IP (VoIP). I live in Colorado with my family. If I'm not coding, I love to spend time with my two children. We enjoy watching Broncos games, skiing, fishing, playing violin, and cooking together. We enjoy all outdoor activities Colorado has to offer.
